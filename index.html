WELCOME TO @BEYAZ-FRAMBUAZ WEBSITE

MOTIVATION

I am currently learning to code with Harvard's CS50 course and they offered to make a website as one of the assignments. I decided to take this opportunity to tell a couple of my memorable stories.

Over the almost-30 (holly ^&%@!#) years of my life I've been blessed with enough adventures, travels, cities and countries I lived at that I can call myself a citizen of this planet. Yet even this website is being drafted pretty far away from my birthplace.

I was born in a small town in central Ukraine. My hometown was a pleasant place to spend a childhood, but as I grew older I realized that the place reeked of stagnation and a peculiar sense of hopelessness, one that can only be experienced by those born and raised there. Anyhow, since my high-school years I dreamt of other places, far away countries, new cities, ..

Dreams did come true and since then I lived in 4 different cities, 2 other countries, climbed a few mountains and tried a ton of food I had no idea existed! Might not sound like too much, but it's not the quantity that matters, rather the path.

This website is a tribute to some of those adventures. It show my hometown of Zhovti Vody, some of my outdoors journeys and the cities I've lived in or visited.
